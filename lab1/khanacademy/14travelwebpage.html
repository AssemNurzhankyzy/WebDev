Below is the list of places you can go:

  1. Almaty - The Southern Capital

    2. The largest city and former capital of Kazakhstan, nestled at the foothills of the Trans-Ili Alatau mountains. Known for its beautiful parks, modern architecture, and as the cultural heart of the country.

  2. Charyn Canyon

    3. Often called "Kazakhstan's Grand Canyon," this spectacular natural wonder features dramatic red rock formations and the stunning "Valley of Castles" section. The canyon stretches approximately 154 km along the Charyn River.

  3. Medeo Skating Rink and Ski Resort

    4. Located in the mountains above Almaty, Medeo is the highest ice skating rink in the world at 1,691 meters above sea level. Nearby Shymbulak Ski Resort offers excellent skiing with breathtaking mountain views.

  4. Burabay (Borovoe) National Park

    5. Known as the "Kazakh Switzerland," this stunning national park features pristine lakes, unique rock formations, and pine forests. It's a popular resort area for both locals and tourists seeking nature and relaxation.

  5. Baikonur Cosmodrome

    6. The world's first and largest operational space launch facility. Visitors can tour the cosmodrome where Yuri Gagarin made his historic first spaceflight and learn about space exploration history.
